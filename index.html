Shared Closet is my personal website for free sharing of clothes that I don't wear/or rarely wear (maybe they're not the right size for me, they're out of fashion for me,). Whenever I buy a piece of clothing, especially fast fashion, I have never considered how many times I will wear it, once, or five times? It relfected an unconscious and incredible textile waste! This is the intention of the SharedCloset, to raise everyone's awareness on textile pollution, especially fast fashion.
